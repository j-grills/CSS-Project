About Prairie View Showbiz
Prairie View is the home of the illustrious Prairie View A&M University. Although our campus is great, we still barely have anything entertaining to do. Showbiz will bring a diverse form of entertainment for the area of Prairie View, Hempstead and all of Waller county.
Prairie View has a lot of free space, with barely places to have fun. In order to build Showbiz, we will need to have a lot of space to host the entertainment. We will also need about $13 Million to build and begin the opening of Showbiz.
