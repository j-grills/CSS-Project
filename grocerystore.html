Welcome to PVAMU Grocery Store

Supermarkets are grocery stores with about $4 million in revenue, with average square feet of 15,000. This grocery store will be independently owned but it is estimated to gather millions in revenue. Students come on campus to start their new journey away from their family and comfort zones. This grocery store is a place where students can find the best best quality products at affordable prices. There is currently no grocery store within at least 20 miles of Prairie View A&M Campus. Placing a store near campus would help out students that are experiencing food insecurities.
