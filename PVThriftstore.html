Welcome to Prairie View Thrifting Center


Come Shop!

Students in Prairie View and Waller County face limitations in accessing resources, particularly due to the area's predominantly student population. Many students struggle financially and must contend with the inconvenience of traveling approximately 30 minutes to obtain essential items. To address this need, we are establishing a thrifting center conveniently located between Prairie View and Waller County. This center will be easily accessible to all students, offering a wide range of affordable items including clothing, furniture, shoes, jewelry, dressers, student desks, chairs, purses, and perfume. Our goal is to provide a one-stop shop for students of Prairie View A&M University, ensuring they have access to quality items without the need for long drives or excessive costs. By reducing travel distances, students will save on gas and have the opportunity to maintain a fresh and stylish appearance at school. Join us in creating a more accessible and affordable shopping experience for all students in the area!
